About Me

I'm Ryan Gildea, a versatile audio professional with over 20 years of experience in audio engineering, mixing, and music production. My journey in sound began with a passion for music and technology, which has evolved into a career dedicated to crafting exceptional audio experiences.

Having worked at Audible as both a software development engineer and manager, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and creative vision to every project. My background in development allows me to approach audio work with precision and innovation, while my artistic sensibilities ensure that each piece tells a compelling story.

When I'm not in the studio, I enjoy developing fullstack web applications and exploring new technologies like serverless functions and static site generators.
