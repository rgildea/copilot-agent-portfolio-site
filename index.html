About Me

I'm Ryan Gildea, a versatile audio professional with over 20 years of experience in audio engineering, mixing, and music production. My journey in sound began with a passion for music and technology, which has evolved into a career dedicated to crafting exceptional audio experiences across multiple genres.

As a former Software Development Engineer and Manager at Audible, I bring a unique perspective to audio production by combining technical precision with artistic creativity. This blend of skills enables me to tackle complex audio challenges and deliver professional results that exceed expectations.

My approach to each project is collaborative and detail-oriented. Whether mixing a full-length album, producing original music, or developing custom audio solutions, I'm committed to realizing your creative vision while maintaining the highest technical standards.

When I'm not in the studio, I enjoy developing fullstack web applications, exploring new audio technologies, and contributing to open-source audio software projects.
